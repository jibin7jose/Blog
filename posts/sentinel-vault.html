Sentinel Vault: Zero-Knowledge Strategic Archival

Briefing v1.0.4 // Founder Clearance Level 9

The Sentinel Vault is the core cryptographic sanctuary within the Oasis Shell. Designed as a zero-knowledge substrate, it ensures that strategic assets—code blueprints, fiscal reports, and neural intents—are encrypted at rest and accessible only through derived founder keys.

Core Working Functionality

  • Neural Key Derivation

    The vault utilizes a Founder Secret combined with a unique workspace salt to derive a master encryption key. No secrets are stored in plain text or persistent memory.

  • Strategic Sealing

    When an asset is "Sealed," the Rust kernel performs an atomic read-encrypt-move sequence. The original file is shredded from the host OS, leaving only an encrypted blob within the Oasis Enclave.

  • Integrity Resonance

    A real-time "Security Resonance" score monitors the stability of the vault ledger, detecting unauthorized modifications or bit-rot within the archival layer.

  • Audit Logging

    Every unseal event is logged with a neural timestamp, creating a non-repudiable audit trail for strategic asset access.

The Interface
