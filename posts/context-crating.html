The Crating Engine

A "Crate" is more than just a list of open apps. It captures the spatial arrangement and strategic relevance of your workspace at a specific moment in time.

  • AI Context Naming: Utilizing the local Gemma3 model, the system analyzes the titles of your open windows to suggest a punchy, conceptual name for the crate.
  • SQLite Persistence: Crates are stored in a local encrypted ledger (oasis_crates.db), ensuring your workspace configurations are available instantly upon boot.
  • Native Spawning: The Rust kernel interacts directly with the OS sub-system to relaunch applications, bypassing the limitations of the web layer.

Strategic Impact

By using Alt + C, you can now toggle between "Design Mode," "Dev Mode," and "Growth Strategy" without ever having to search for a task-bar icon. It is the ultimate expression of the Oasis Shell's philosophy: Focus is Currency.
